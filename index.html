My Favorite Takeaways

My goal with this website was to have a place to store some of my personal favorite stories and resources. Aside from Come Follow Me, I haven't really done an in-depth study specficially focused on the New Testament, so I really enjoyed diving in more with this class. I organized this website with a couple pages. The last page also has a survey so that anyone who visits the site can share their thoughts as well. The survey touches on favorite stories, favorite pictures, and any other thoughts people want to share. If you want to take it, scan the code below, or head over to the survey page!
